Posted On October 12, 2014 At 12:18 Pm By Jhon

Kingston Educational Institute, the stepping stone for the students to enter into the kingdom of higher education has completed its 12 years of its glorious existence on serving humanity. The sprawling campus covering an area of 30 acres of land is located in a semi-urban place surrounded by lush green meadow in the serenity of pollution free and eco-friendly environment. Veteran teachers with their quality teaching have been the greatest strength of the KEI which has helps the thousands of students to bear the light of hope and secured future.
